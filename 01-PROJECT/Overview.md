# Advisor Intelligence — Overview

#project #overview

## What It Is
Advisor Intelligence is the AI layer for everything a financial advisor's existing systems don't cover. It provides **copy-paste templates** organized around the advisor's daily workflow — meeting prep, follow-ups, client emails, prospecting, and content creation. Also includes a **Document Builder**, **How-To Guides**, **Action Plan scenarios**, and an **AI Coach** chatbot.

The core model is **copy-paste**: advisors select a template, it auto-injects their profile data, they copy it to their clipboard, and paste it into their firm's approved AI tool (Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, or Claude). Client data never enters the platform.

## Positioning (updated 2026-03-14)
**"Your planning software builds the plan. Your CRM stores the data. We handle everything in between."**

Advisors already have dedicated systems for portfolio management (Orion, Black Diamond), financial planning (eMoney, RightCapital), tax analysis (Holistiplan), and risk assessment (Riskalyze). They will NOT use AI for those functions. They WILL use AI for the gaps between their systems: meeting prep, follow-up emails, CRM notes, client communication, prospecting, and content creation. Advisor Intelligence fills those gaps.
