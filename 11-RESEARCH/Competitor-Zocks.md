# Zocks

## Overview
Zocks is a privacy-first AI meeting assistant built specifically for financial advisors. Founded in 2022, headquartered in San Francisco, CA. Unique differentiator: captures meeting intelligence WITHOUT recording conversations — creates real-time notes during meetings rather than transcribing recordings after. SOC 2 Type II certified. Trusted by 5,000+ advisory firms.

## Pricing
- **Individual**: $150/month per user (unlimited meetings)
- **Firm**: $130/user/month (volume discount, unlimited meetings)
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for larger organizations
- Unlike competitors, Zocks places no limits on monthly meetings
- Annual equivalent: $1,560-$1,800/user/year

## Target Market & Firm Size Sweet Spot
- Independent advisory firms (primary)
- Enterprise clients with compliance requirements
- Advisors who value privacy and can't/won't record client meetings
- Firms with in-person meeting cultures (not just virtual)
- Compliance-conscious firms where recording is prohibited

## Product Features
- Real-time meeting note capture (no recording required)
- Detailed meeting summaries formatted to advisor specifications
- CRM integration and automatic updates (Redtail, Wealthbox, Salesforce)
- Future meeting scheduling and calendar booking
- Pre-meeting prep with agenda and "cheat sheet" from prior meetings
- Client Profiles: coalescing data across meetings into centralized view
- Form auto-fill from meeting data
- Follow-up email drafting
- In-person, virtual, and phone call support
- Unlimited meetings (no monthly caps on plans)
- Integration with Holistiplan for tax context
- SOC 2 Type II certified, data residency controls

## Messaging & Positioning

## Strengths (what they do better than advisorintelligence.app)
- Privacy-first AI approach resonates with compliance teams

## Weaknesses & Gaps (what advisors complain about)
- Meeting-based pricing can get expensive for high-volume advisors ($0.99/meeting overages)
- No independent negative reviews found — too new for complaint aggregation
- Narrow focus: only meeting workflows, no content creation or marketing help
- 446 LinkedIn followers suggests limited brand awareness beyond users
- No templates for prospecting, client education, marketing, or practice management
- Competes in increasingly crowded AI notetaker space (Jump, Fireflies, Nitrogen's notetaker, etc.)

## How Advisor Intelligence Differentiates
1. **Content creation vs. meeting automation** — Zocks automates meeting workflows; we help advisors create compelling communications, marketing materials, and client education content.
2. **Radically cheaper** — $600/year (unlimited) vs $800-$1,300/year (meeting-limited). No per-meeting overages.
3. **No meeting dependency** — Our templates work independently of any meeting. Zocks requires actual client conversations to generate value.
4. **Broader scope** — 11 categories covering the full advisor practice. Zocks only covers the meeting lifecycle.
5. **Complementary** — Advisors using Zocks for meetings still need templates for prospecting emails, client education pieces, and marketing content.
