### Services Offered
1. **Investment Management Services**
   - Discuss investment goals, recommend one of three strategies or custom strategy

   - Discretionary and non-discretionary account options
   - Limited selection of investments (focused, not broad-market)
   - **Minimum investment: $300,000** (waivable at firm discretion)

2. **Financial Planning Services**
   - In-depth personal interviews, financial document review, questionnaires
   - Written plan or other format recommendations
   - One-time delivery (no ongoing monitoring unless client requests update)

- **Asset management**: Percentage of AUM, deducted directly from account, paid quarterly
- **Financial planning**: Hourly fee, due upon delivery of recommendations

# DLK Pain Points and How We Solve Them

## Pain Point 1: High Client-to-Advisor Ratio (1:78)
**Problem**: 6 advisors serve 470 clients. Each advisor handles significant communication volume -- quarterly reviews, ad hoc questions, life event responses, prospecting.
**Solution**: Our template library (61 templates) covers client communication, prospecting, life events, and more. Each template auto-injects advisor profile data and generates ready-to-send content in minutes, not hours.

## Pain Point 2: Multi-State Operations from One Office
**Problem**: Clients in 9 states, all served from Solana Beach. Remote communication is the norm, not the exception.
**Solution**: Our client communication templates are designed for remote delivery -- email templates, follow-up sequences, and check-in frameworks that maintain personal touch at distance.

## Pain Point 3: Compliance Culture Without Compliance Tooling
**Problem**: Founded by an ex-SEC accountant, DLK values compliance deeply. But compliance review of every piece of client communication is a bottleneck.
**Solution**: Every Advisor Intelligence template includes SEC/FINRA compliance guardrails. Advisors can generate compliant content without routing every email through CCO review.

## Pain Point 4: Financial Planning Content Generation
**Problem**: Two CFPs (Mark, Tom) do comprehensive financial planning -- estate, retirement, tax. Creating personalized plans, summaries, and client education materials is time-intensive.
**Solution**: Our estate_planning, retirement_planning, and tax_planning categories (15+ templates) generate plan summaries, client education pieces, and action items in minutes.

## Pain Point 5: No AI Workflow Standardization
**Problem**: If DLK advisors are using AI at all, each person likely uses ChatGPT/Copilot differently, with inconsistent quality and no compliance oversight.
**Solution**: Advisor Intelligence provides a standardized, compliance-aware template library that ensures consistent quality across the entire team.
