# Key Takeaways for Advisor Intelligence
1. Kwanti proves that small, focused tools win advisor satisfaction — highest satisfaction rating among all investment analytics tools despite being a tiny company (2-10 employees). Our focused approach can work.
2. At $195-$575/month, Kwanti is 4-12x our price for a narrow analytics use case. Reinforces our value message: $49.99/mo covers 11 categories.
3. Kwanti has zero overlap with our product — they analyze portfolios; we help advisors communicate. Pure complementary positioning.

# Kwanti

## Overview
Kwanti is a web-based portfolio analytics platform for financial advisors and investment managers. Founded in 2008, headquartered in San Francisco, CA, with 2-10 employees. Offers institutional-caliber analytics including portfolio drill-down, stress tests, performance attribution, risk analysis, optimization, and client proposal generation. Data sourced from Morningstar.

## Pricing (Confirmed 2026)
- **Two editions**: $195/month to $575/month per advisor
- Volume discounts available for multiple licenses
- Free 30-day trial available
- FINRA-reviewed reports included
- License terms: per advisor
- Among the lowest-priced investment analytics tools in the market despite high satisfaction

## Target Market & Firm Size Sweet Spot
- Data-driven financial advisors who do their own investment research
- Solo practitioners and small RIAs who manage their own models
- Advisors who want Morningstar-quality analytics at lower cost
- Investment managers running model portfolios
- Available for larger RIAs, broker-dealers, and advisor networks

## Product Features
- Portfolio allocation drill-down
- Stress testing and scenario analysis
- Performance attribution
- Risk analysis
- Hypothetical portfolio modeling
- Portfolio optimization
- Blended benchmarks
- Custom client proposals and reports
- Built-in PDF report editor
- Risk-profiling tools and questionnaires
- Investment screening ("Screener" feature)
- Client risk tolerance vs. portfolio alignment comparison
- Integrations with performance reporting software and custodians

## Messaging & Positioning
- "Smart Analytics for Data-Driven Advisors"
- Positioned as affordable alternative to Morningstar's full platform
- Emphasis on ease of use with institutional-caliber depth
- Professional, data-focused tone
- Testimonials page features advisor quotes about analytics quality

## Trust Signals & Social Proof
- Highest satisfaction rating among all investment analytics tools (Kitces Research)

- Listed in Kitces FinTech Directory

- Among the lowest-priced investment analytics tools

## Strengths (what they do better than advisorintelligence.app)
- Deep quantitative portfolio analytics with Morningstar data
- FINRA-reviewed report templates
- 18 years of product maturity and advisor trust
- Highest satisfaction in category despite being among cheapest
- Direct custodian integrations for live portfolio data
- Institutional-quality stress testing and optimization

## Weaknesses & Gaps (what advisors complain about)
- No specific complaints found — very small user base limits review volume
- 2-10 employees means limited feature velocity and customer support capacity
- Narrow scope: investment analytics only
- No AI capabilities, no meeting automation, no content creation
- No mobile app mentioned
- $195-$575/month may still be expensive for solo advisors who only need basic analytics
- Limited integration ecosystem compared to larger platforms

## How Advisor Intelligence Differentiates
1. **Zero overlap** — Kwanti analyzes investment portfolios. We help advisors write emails, create marketing content, prospect, and communicate. Completely different tools for different problems.
2. **4x cheaper at minimum** — $49.99/mo vs $195/mo starting price.
3. **Broader utility** — 11 categories covering the full advisor practice vs. investment analytics only.
4. **No data requirements** — Kwanti needs portfolio data linked to custodians. We work with zero data connections.
5. **Complementary** — Advisors using Kwanti for portfolio analysis still need templates to communicate those insights to clients. That's what we do.
