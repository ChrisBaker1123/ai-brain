# Brand Voice

#content #brand

## Voice Characteristics
1. **Professional but human** — Not corporate jargon, not casual slang
2. **Confident without being salesy** — State facts, don't hype
3. **Educational** — Teach, don't lecture
4. **Direct** — Say what you mean, no filler
5. **Compliance-aware** — Acknowledge regulatory reality without fearmongering
6. **Honest** — Never fake metrics, testimonials, or credentials

## In the Brand Voice ✓
1. "Your personal AI coach for your advisory practice."
2. "60+ copy-paste templates. Compliance references included."
3. "Free during early access — no credit card required."
4. "We're compliance-aware, not compliance-approved."
5. "Proof, not promises."
6. "Works with your firm's AI tools, not instead of them."
7. "Your data stays yours. Templates copy to your clipboard."
8. "Built for advisors who want to use AI without the guesswork."
9. "No prompt engineering required. Copy, paste, done."
10. "We don't replace your judgment — we save you time."

## NOT in the Brand Voice ✗
1. "Revolutionize your practice with cutting-edge AI!" (too hype)
2. "Join 500+ advisors already using our platform!" (fake social proof)
3. "Our AI-powered solution leverages..." (corporate jargon)
4. "Don't get left behind in the AI revolution!" (fear-based)
5. "Guaranteed to increase your AUM!" (compliance violation)
6. "The most advanced AI toolkit on the market!" (unsubstantiated claim)
7. "Hey there! 👋 Ready to crush it?" (too casual, emoji)
8. "Our proprietary algorithms..." (we don't have proprietary AI)
9. "Trusted by top advisors nationwide!" (no real users yet)
10. "Schedule a call to learn more about our enterprise solutions!" (not an enterprise play)

## Key Rules
- **Never fabricate user counts or testimonials** — No real users yet, and fake social proof destroys trust
- **Never use "AI-powered" as a feature** — The templates ARE the product; AI is the tool advisors paste into
- **Always distinguish "compliance-aware" from "compliance-approved"** — We reference regulations, we don't provide legal advice
- **Use "templates" not "prompts"** — User-facing language convention
- **Copilot-first** — Primary AI tool reference is Microsoft Copilot, not ChatGPT
- **Be specific** — "61 templates across 11 categories" beats "dozens of templates"

## Tone Variations by Context
| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Landing page | Confident, clear | "Your personal AI coach for your advisory practice." |
| Trust center | Reassuring, precise | "Client data never enters our platform." |
| Pricing | Transparent, no-pressure | "Free during early access. No strings attached." |
| Error messages | Helpful, brief | "Something went wrong. Please try again." |
| Onboarding | Welcoming, guided | "Let's personalize your toolkit." |
| Templates | Professional, instructive | "Copy this to your clipboard, then paste into Copilot." |
