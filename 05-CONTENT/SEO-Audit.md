## Missing SEO Elements
1. **No structured data** — FAQ pages should have FAQ schema markup
2. **No og:image** — Social sharing will lack visual preview
3. **No canonical URLs** — Could cause duplicate content issues
4. **No sitemap.xml** — Search engines can't efficiently crawl
5. **No robots.txt** — No crawl directives
6. **No alt text audit** — Images may lack proper alt attributes
