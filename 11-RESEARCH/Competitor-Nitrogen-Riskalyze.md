# Nitrogen (formerly Riskalyze)

## Overview
Nitrogen (rebranded from Riskalyze in May 2023) is an AI-powered client engagement platform trusted by 30,000+ financial advisors. Founded in 2011 by Aaron Klein, originally as a risk tolerance assessment tool using a Nobel Prize-winning framework. Now positions as a "growth platform" for wealth management firms with three product centers: Risk, Research, and Planning.

## Pricing
- **Riskalyze plan** (risk tolerance + proposals): $99/month (re-launched as standalone after unbundling)
- **Nitrogen Complete** (full platform — risk + research + planning + premium support): higher tiers, not publicly disclosed
- **Team Solutions**: accounts-based pricing for growing RIAs (pricing by number of accounts in data feed, not users)
- Annual commitment required
- A la carte product access available (Risk Center, Research Center, Planning Center as separate products)
- Kitces noted previous full-platform price at $395/user/month — "advisors seeking a risk tool primarily for compliance may have balked"

## Target Market & Firm Size Sweet Spot
- Broad market: solo advisors to large RIA firms
- Core audience: advisors who lead with risk assessment in client conversations
- Growing into: financial planning and investment research users
- Enterprise: firms wanting accounts-based pricing
- Deep wirehouse and broker-dealer penetration alongside RIAs

## Product Features
- **Nitrogen Risk Center**: Risk tolerance questionnaires, proposal generation, client engagement tools
- **Nitrogen Research Center**: Investment research, model management, Fund Intersection for equity exposure analysis
- **Nitrogen Planning Center**: Accumulation and distribution modeling
- **AI Statement Capture**: Converts client statements into actionable portfolios
- **Stats Multiview**: Side-by-side portfolio comparisons
- **Native AI Meeting Notetaker**: Recently launched embedded meeting notes tool
- Integrations with major CRMs, custodians, and portfolio management platforms

## Messaging & Positioning
- "The growth platform for wealth management firms"
- "Intrigue leads into meetings, turn meetings into valued clients, and clients into referral champions"
- Annual "Fearless Investing Summit" conference reinforces bold, growth-oriented brand
- Shifted language from "risk assessment" to "growth" and "fearless" — aspirational, not defensive
- CTA emphasis on growth, not compliance or caution

## Trust Signals & Social Proof

## Strengths (what they do better than advisorintelligence.app)
- 15 years of brand equity and advisor trust
- 30,000+ advisor base with strong retention

- Moving into AI meeting notes shows willingness to expand

## Weaknesses & Gaps (what advisors complain about)
- **Risk methodology questioned**: "Coin toss questionnaire... vastly different risk scores than other methods... do not trust Riskalyze methodology" (G2 review)
- **Software performance**: "Extremely slow... several minutes to load one page" (Capterra review)
- **Billing practices**: Charged users for months not used; contract exit only possible for 30 days per year; users stuck paying if they miss cancellation window (BBB complaints)
- **Customer service**: "Curt and unwilling to listen when issues about incorrect risk/return numbers were raised" (review)
- **A- BBB rating with 6 complaints filed**
- **Narrow core expertise**: Risk/proposal tools don't help with content creation, marketing, or client communication
- No templates for client emails, prospecting, education, or practice management

## How Advisor Intelligence Differentiates
1. **Communication and content vs. risk assessment** — Nitrogen tells advisors what risk score a client has; we help advisors communicate effectively about planning topics, prospect new clients, and create marketing content.
2. **No lock-in** — Our month-to-month at $49.99 vs their annual contracts starting at $99/mo with restrictive cancellation windows.
3. **Zero learning curve** — No questionnaire methodology to learn, no portfolio data to import. Just copy-paste templates.
4. **Works WITH their tools** — Advisors using Nitrogen for risk assessment still need help writing the follow-up email, creating marketing materials, and educating clients. We complement Nitrogen.
5. **Broader use case** — 11 categories covering the full advisor practice vs. risk/proposals/research.
