# San Diego Top 20 RIA Prospects

> Last updated: 2026-03-13
> Data sources: SEC Form ADV filings, SmartAsset, AdvizorPro, firm websites, LinkedIn, web research

## Key Takeaways

1. **Mid-market firms ($250M-$1B AUM) are the sweet spot** — Large enough to need efficiency tools, small enough to lack dedicated technology teams. Focus outreach on Tier 2-3 firms first.
2. **Fee-only firms are more likely to adopt** — Fee-only culture in San Diego aligns with our transparent, fiduciary-first positioning. These firms value tools that enhance client outcomes.
3. **Firms with 3-20 advisors are ideal** — Large enough to benefit from standardized templates, small enough that a single decision-maker can approve the purchase.
4. **Post-acquisition firms are in transition** — HoyleCohen (Colony Group), Laurel Wealth (Integrated Partners), and Callan Capital (AlphaCore) are all navigating integration. They may be looking for tools to standardize workflows.
5. **DLK Investment Management is our warmest lead** — Already in pipeline, Solana Beach location, 11 employees, $348M AUM. Follow up is ~March 16, 2026.
