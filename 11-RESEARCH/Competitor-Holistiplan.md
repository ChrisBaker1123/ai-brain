# Holistiplan

## Overview
Holistiplan is an award-winning tax planning software founded in 2019 by two CFP professionals (Roger Pine and Kevin Lozer). It uses OCR technology to scan client tax returns in under 60 seconds, generating white-labeled reports that surface tax planning opportunities like Roth conversions, charitable giving strategies, and tax-efficient withdrawals. They have expanded into estate planning document extraction and P&C insurance risk analysis.

## Pricing
- Previous model: per-advisor pricing (specifics undisclosed)
- 2025 restructuring: shifted to household-based pricing, nearly doubling costs for many firms
- **Tiered by volume**: 30 households = $749/yr (~$62/mo) | 50 returns = $449/yr (~$37/mo) | 150 households = ~$2,000/yr | 450 households = $6,849/yr | 5,000 returns = $30,000/yr
- Starting price: ~$449/year for 50 returns
- All plans: 7-day free trial, annual pricing saves ~16% vs monthly
- Price increase rollout: existing customers grandfathered until renewal, with a 20% loyalty discount in year one if increase exceeds 25%
- Non-refundable annual commitment

## Target Market & Firm Size Sweet Spot
- Independent RIAs and fee-only advisors who want to add tax planning value
- Firm sizes: solo practitioners to mid-size RIAs (1-50 advisors)
- Heaviest adoption among CFP professionals who want to demonstrate tax alpha
- Also targets CPA firms offering advisory services
- Schwab Advisor Services partner (listed in their provider directory)

## Product Features
- OCR tax return scanning (processes returns in <60 seconds)
- White-labeled tax summary reports for clients
- Scenario modeling (Roth conversions, charitable giving, withdrawals)
- Estate planning document extraction tool (launched late 2024)
- Insurance Risk Management Tool with home rebuilding cost estimator
- P&C coverage gap identification
- Integration with Jump, PreciseFP, Zocks

## Messaging & Positioning
- Homepage headline: "We bring tax planning to every advisor so they can bring it to every client."
- Positioning: making tax planning accessible and scalable, not just for tax specialists
- Language emphasis: "comprehensive," "award-winning," "trusted by 30,000+"
- Value prop: "long-term strategies transcend tax season so advisors can grow their clients' wallet share"
- Strong emphasis on speed — "45 seconds" to process a tax return

- 42% adoption rate among all advisors (Kitces Research)

## Strengths (what they do better than advisorintelligence.app)
- Deep, specialized tax analysis that actually reads and processes client documents
- 5 years of market leadership with massive adoption (42% of all advisors)
- Produces client-ready deliverables (white-labeled reports)
- Integration ecosystem with CRMs, planning tools, and AI notetakers
- Strong Kitces endorsement and conference presence
- Quantifiable advisor ROI: "surfaces X tax savings opportunities per client"

## Weaknesses & Gaps (what advisors complain about)
- Price nearly doubled in 2025 — significant advisor backlash reported on Kitces
- No Schedule E rental property analysis (user-requested feature gap)
- No state tax analysis reporting
- Narrow focus: tax/estate/insurance only — no help with client communication, prospecting, marketing, or content creation
- Requires uploading sensitive client tax documents to their platform
- New household-based pricing model confuses firms with varying client counts
- Estate planning tool is new and less mature than tax module

## How Advisor Intelligence Differentiates
1. **Zero data upload required** — Our copy-paste model means client tax returns never touch our servers. Holistiplan requires uploading PDFs of sensitive tax documents.
2. **10x broader scope** — We cover 11 categories including prospecting, client communication, marketing content, and practice management. Holistiplan only does tax/estate/insurance analysis.
3. **70% cheaper** — At $49.99/mo vs their ~$167/mo starting price, we're a fraction of the cost.
4. **Different use case entirely** — Holistiplan analyzes documents; we help advisors communicate, create content, and run their practice with AI. We complement Holistiplan, not compete head-on.
5. **No learning curve** — Holistiplan requires understanding OCR workflows and scenario modeling. Our templates are copy-paste ready.
