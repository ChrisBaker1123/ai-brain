## Direct Email Templates

### Cold Email 1: Problem-Aware
**Subject**: "Quick question about your AI workflow"
**Body**: 
"Hi [Name], I noticed [firm] is in [city] — I've been working with independent RIAs on a specific problem: the gap between knowing AI could save time and actually using it effectively.

We built a toolkit of 60+ copy-paste templates designed specifically for advisors. You paste them into Copilot or ChatGPT and get polished, compliance-aware output in under 2 minutes.

It's free right now. Would you be open to a 15-minute walkthrough?

Christopher Baker
Advisor Intelligence"

### Cold Email 2: Value-First
**Subject**: "Free AI template for [specific use case]"
**Body**:
"Hi [Name], I put together an AI template for [meeting prep / client emails / tax planning] that advisors at [firm type] firms are finding useful.

Here's how it works: copy the template, paste into your firm's AI tool, fill in your client's details, and you get a polished [output type] in about 2 minutes.

I can send you the template directly — no signup required. Interested?

Christopher Baker"

## LinkedIn Outreach Sequence

### Connection Request
"Hi [Name] — I'm building AI tools for financial advisors and would love to connect. I post regularly about practical AI use cases for advisory practices."

### Follow-up (Day 3)
"Thanks for connecting! Quick question — does your firm use any AI tools for client communications or meeting prep? I'm curious what advisors are finding useful."

### Value Drop (Day 7)
"I mentioned I build AI templates for advisors — here's one that's been popular: [template name]. It turns [task] from a 15-minute process into 2 minutes. Happy to share a few more if useful."
