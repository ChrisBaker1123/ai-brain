**Target audience**: Advisors who want a quick, actionable AI workflow
**Target keyword**: financial advisor email templates AI

### Post Text

How to write a client follow-up email in 90 seconds using AI:

Step 1: Open a pre-built template designed for your scenario.

Instead of staring at a blank ChatGPT window, start with a template that already knows:
- Your firm type (RIA, BD, hybrid)
- The meeting context (annual review, estate planning, onboarding)
- The compliance guardrails for your situation

Step 2: Copy the template to your clipboard.

One click. The template auto-fills with your firm details. Anything it can't fill becomes a clear instruction like [INSERT CLIENT NAME] so you know exactly what to customize.

Step 3: Paste into Microsoft Copilot or ChatGPT.

The template includes specific instructions for the AI: tone, length, regulatory boundaries, what NOT to say. You get output that sounds like you, not like a robot.

Step 4: Review for 30 seconds and send.

That follow-up email that used to take 15-25 minutes? Done before your coffee gets cold.
