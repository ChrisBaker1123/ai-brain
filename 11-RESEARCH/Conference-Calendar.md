# 2026 Advisor Conference Calendar

> Last updated: 2026-03-13
> Sources: [Kitces Master List](https://www.kitces.com/master-list-top-financial-advisor-conferences-ranked-best/), [InvestmentNews](https://www.investmentnews.com/practice-management/ria-conferences-usa-2026/264991), [RIA Channel](https://www.riachannel.com/conference-calendar/), [AdvizorPro](https://advizorpro.com/post/financial-conferences-calendar)

## Key Takeaways for Advisor Intelligence

1. **Two major conferences are coming to San Diego** — XYPN LIVE (Sept 29-Oct 1) and FPA Shift (April 20-22 in La Jolla). These are must-attend events where Christopher Baker can demo the product locally without travel costs.
2. **T3 Technology Conference is the #1 wealthtech event** — March 9-12 in New Orleans. This is where advisor tech buying decisions are influenced. If we can attend/exhibit at one national event, this should be it.
3. **The conference circuit peaks in March/April and September/October** — Plan outreach campaigns around these windows when advisors are thinking about new tools.
4. **AI is now a standalone track** — T3 launched an "AI University" track, and multiple conferences feature AI-specific programming. Position Advisor Intelligence in this conversation.
5. **Regional and niche conferences offer better ROI for startups** — FPA chapter events, NAPFA study groups, and smaller gatherings often have more accessible attendees and lower costs than flagship events.
