# Key Takeaways for Advisor Intelligence
1. FP Alpha's $1,795-$1,995/yr price point and credit-based model creates friction and unpredictability — our flat $49.99/mo unlimited model is radically simpler and cheaper.
2. Their positioning as "Where AI Meets Financial Planning" is the closest to our language in the market — we need to differentiate clearly that we're about AI-powered CONTENT and COMMUNICATION, not document analysis.
3. FP Alpha covers 16 planning disciplines but requires uploading client documents (tax returns, wills, insurance policies). Our zero-upload model is a major compliance advantage.

# FP Alpha

## Overview
FP Alpha is an AI-driven comprehensive wealth management platform founded by Andrew Altfest (president of Altfest Personal Wealth Management) and Luis Quiroz. The platform "reads" clients' tax returns, wills, trusts, and insurance policies, then summarizes data, identifies actionable planning insights, and quantifies the value of advice across 16 planning disciplines. Winner of WealthManagement.com's 2022 Innovation Award.

## Pricing (Updated 2026)
- **All-in-one license**: $1,995/year per advisor (tax + estate + P&C insurance planning)
- **Estate planning only**: $1,790/year per advisor
- **FP Alpha Tax standalone**: $1,125 for first 75 tax uploads (launched Jan 2025)
- Credit-based model: each tax document review = 1 credit; each estate household review = 1 credit
- Credits expire and reload annually; additional credits available in packs of 10
- Per-case equivalent: $13-$15 per tax analysis case
- Office/team packages at higher tiers for multi-advisor firms

## Target Market & Firm Size Sweet Spot
- Mid-to-large RIAs and advisory teams who do advanced planning
- Firms wanting to scale tax + estate + insurance analysis without hiring specialists
- CPA firms and estate attorneys (secondary market)
- Enterprise wealth management firms with multiple offices
- Schwab Advisor Services partner

## Product Features
- **Tax Planning**: AI-driven tax return scanning, missed opportunity identification, Roth conversion modeling
- **Estate Planning**: Estate Insights 2.0 with AI-driven document analysis, redesigned questionnaire workflow, categorized observations
- **Insurance Planning**: P&C coverage evaluation, liability gap identification, policy simplification
- **Prospecting**: "Financial Wellness" score for prospects based on planning ideas checklist
- **NextGen Tax Insights**: Analyzes tax return + financial data to surface planning opportunities from a vetted strategy list
- **Tax Wrap**: Proprietary feature for comprehensive tax analysis
- **Integrations**: Redtail, Wealthbox, Salesforce, eMoney, MoneyGuidePro, open API

## Strengths (what they do better than advisorintelligence.app)
- Covers 16 planning disciplines — genuinely comprehensive document analysis
- AI actually reads and interprets complex legal/financial documents (wills, trusts, policies)
- Quantifies dollar value of planning recommendations (hard ROI for advisors)
- Strong integration ecosystem with major CRMs and planning platforms
- Estate planning capabilities are genuinely sophisticated
- Enterprise pricing and team packages for larger firms

## Weaknesses & Gaps (what advisors complain about)
- Credit-based pricing creates friction — advisors have to think about whether each analysis is "worth a credit"
- Credits expire annually — use-it-or-lose-it model frustrates lower-volume advisors
- Steep learning curve — understanding 16 planning disciplines takes time
- 3.94% market share vs Holistiplan's 32% — still proving product-market fit
- Requires uploading sensitive client documents (tax returns, wills, insurance policies)
- No content creation, client communication, or marketing capabilities
- Complex pricing structure confuses solo advisors

## How Advisor Intelligence Differentiates
1. **Content creation vs. document analysis** — FP Alpha reads documents and surfaces insights; we help advisors write emails, create marketing content, communicate with clients, and prospect. Completely different value propositions.
2. **Zero client data upload** — FP Alpha requires wills, tax returns, and insurance policies. We never touch client data.
3. **75% cheaper** — $49.99/mo ($600/yr) vs $1,795-$1,995/yr. No credit limits, no expiration.
4. **Zero learning curve** — Our templates are ready to copy-paste. FP Alpha requires learning a complex platform across 16 disciplines.
5. **Complementary positioning** — Advisors could use FP Alpha for document analysis AND Advisor Intelligence for communication/content. We're additive, not competitive.
