# San Diego RIA Landscape Map

> Last updated: 2026-03-13

## Key Takeaways

1. **San Diego is a dense, affluent RIA market** — Over 10,000 financial advisors operate in the greater San Diego area, with 200+ SEC-registered RIA firms. The coastal corridor (La Jolla, Del Mar, Solana Beach, Carlsbad) is especially concentrated with high-AUM, fee-only firms serving HNW clients.
2. **A few mega-firms dominate by AUM, but most firms are small** — Chandler Asset Management ($41.6B), Gurtin Municipal Bond/PIMCO ($38B), and Pure Financial Advisors ($8B) are outliers. The majority of independent RIAs manage $100M-$1B and employ 3-20 people.
3. **M&A activity is reshaping the local landscape** — Laurel Wealth Advisors ($2.25B, Solana Beach) acquired by Integrated Partners; HoyleCohen ($3.6B) acquired by Colony Group/Focus Financial; Callan Capital merged with AlphaCore Wealth. This creates both disruption and opportunity.
4. **Strong fee-only culture** — San Diego has an unusually high concentration of fee-only, fiduciary firms, many with NAPFA membership. NAPFA's Fall 2026 conference is in San Diego, signaling local importance.
5. **Technology adoption varies widely** — Larger firms (Pure Financial, Beacon Pointe, Centura) likely have sophisticated tech stacks, while smaller boutiques in La Jolla and Solana Beach are prime targets for our toolkit.

### Key Industry Characteristics

- **Client demographics:** Retirees, military (Navy/Marine), biotech/tech executives, real estate investors, cross-border (Mexico)
- **Specialties common locally:** Retirement planning, biotech equity compensation, military benefits, cross-border tax, sustainable/ESG investing

## Geographic Clusters

### La Jolla (92037, 92038)
**Profile:** Ultra-high-net-worth enclave, highest concentration of boutique wealth managers
**Notable Firms:**
- Callan Capital (merged with AlphaCore, ~$2.4B AUA)
- Canter Wealth (fee-only, voted Best Wealth Management by SD Magazine)
- Nautilus Advisors (SEC-registered, retail investors)
- GROW Funds (RIA)
- Laurel Wealth Advisors (founded in La Jolla)

**Outreach angle:** Sophisticated clientele demands sophisticated tools. These firms serve executives and entrepreneurs who expect personalized, polished communications.

### Del Mar / Solana Beach (92014, 92075)
**Profile:** Affluent coastal community, mix of established firms and newer entrants
**Notable Firms:**
- DLK Investment Management ($348M AUM, 11 employees, Solana Beach) *[active prospect]*
- Laurel Wealth Advisors ($2.25B, acquired by Integrated Partners)

**Outreach angle:** Smaller firms in this area are often independent-minded and looking for tools that help them compete with larger competitors without adding headcount.

### Carmel Valley / Rancho Bernardo (92130, 92127, 92128)
**Profile:** Suburban growth area, mix of mid-size RIAs and national firm branch offices
**Notable Firms:**

**Notable Firms:**

### Sorrento Valley / University City (92121, 92122)

**Notable Firms:**

**Outreach angle:** Firms near biotech companies serve clients with complex equity compensation. Our equity compensation and tax planning templates are directly relevant.

### Carlsbad / North County (92008, 92009, 92011)
