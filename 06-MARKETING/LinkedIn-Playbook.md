# LinkedIn Playbook

#marketing #linkedin

## Why LinkedIn
- ~300K financial advisors in the US, most active on LinkedIn
- Professional context = trust-building opportunity
- Content can be repurposed for blog/email
- Free (organic) reach still viable

## Content Themes
1. **AI education** — Teach advisors what AI can/can't do
2. **Time savings** — Concrete examples of hours saved
3. **Compliance safety** — Address the #1 fear
4. **Template previews** — Show real output examples
5. **Industry commentary** — AI trends in financial services
6. **Behind the scenes** — Building the product, founder story

## Post Frameworks (10)

### 1. Problem → Solution
"Every advisor I talk to says the same thing: [problem]. Here's what we built to fix it: [solution]. [CTA]"

### 2. Before/After
"Before: [old way, time spent]. After: [new way with templates]. The difference: [specific metric]."

### 3. Myth Buster
"Myth: [common misconception about AI for advisors]. Reality: [truth]. Here's what I mean: [example]."

### 4. Template Preview
"Here's a template our advisors use for [use case]: [screenshot or excerpt]. Copy it, paste into Copilot, done in 2 minutes."

### 5. Compliance Angle
"Your compliance team is worried about AI? So were we. That's why every template includes [specific compliance feature]."

### 6. Quick Tip
"Quick AI tip for advisors: [actionable insight]. Try this today: [specific action]."

### 7. Number/Stat
"[Number] — that's how many [metric]. Here's what it means for your practice: [insight]."

### 8. Question Hook
"If you could automate ONE task in your practice, what would it be? For most advisors I talk to, it's [answer]."

### 9. Story
"Last week, an advisor told me [anecdote]. It reminded me why we built [feature]."

### 10. Listicle
"5 things every advisor should know about AI in 2026: 1. [point] 2. [point]..."

## Posting Schedule
- **Frequency**: 3-5x per week
- **Best times**: Tue-Thu, 7-9am or 12-1pm (advisor lunch breaks)
- **Mix**: 60% educational, 20% product, 20% personal/commentary

## Engagement Tactics
- Comment on advisor posts about AI, productivity, practice management
- Join advisor LinkedIn groups
- Tag relevant advisors in educational content (with permission)
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours
- Share others' content with added commentary

## Profile Optimization (Christopher Baker)
- Headline: Clear value prop for advisors
- Banner: Advisor Intelligence branding
- Featured section: Best posts + product link
- About: Story of why he built this

## Metrics
- Impressions per post
- Engagement rate (likes + comments / impressions)
- Profile views from target audience
- Inbound DMs from advisors
- Signups attributed to LinkedIn
