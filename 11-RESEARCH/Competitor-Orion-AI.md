# Key Takeaways for Advisor Intelligence
1. Orion's Denali AI is partnered with Anthropic (Claude's maker) and aims to be the enterprise AI layer for wealth management — their scale (enterprise firms, C-suite dashboards) is completely different from our SMB/solo advisor target.
2. Orion's internal chaos (2.4/5 Glassdoor, 70% executive turnover, offshoring data teams) suggests their AI promises may outpace execution. Advisors frustrated with Orion's delivery speed may be looking for simpler tools.
3. Their minimum ~$15,000/year commitment prices out the solo advisors and small RIAs we target. We own the $50/month segment.

# Orion AI (Denali)

## Overview
Orion is a wealthtech platform serving financial advisors with portfolio management, reporting, billing, compliance, trading, and financial planning tools. In 2026, Orion launched Denali AI, an enterprise AI assistant that brings firm-approved, permissioned data together across Orion's full platform. CEO Natalie Wolfsen has mandated that all Orion staff have "AI goals" for 2026. Orion has expanded its AI collaboration with Anthropic.

## Pricing
- Enterprise custom pricing (not publicly disclosed for bundled stacks)
- **Risk Intelligence standalone**: $275/user/month (stress tests, portfolio construction, 3D risk profile, proposal builder)
- **Essentials Stack**: Bundled portfolio accounting + trading + client experience — custom proposal
- **Advantage Stack**: Most comprehensive bundle (accounting, trading, Risk Intelligence, compliance, more) — custom proposal
- **Denali AI**: Flat monthly subscription, price "adjusted over time as firm incorporates real usage patterns" — exact cost not disclosed
- Mobile/tablet Denali AI apps included with subscription
- Minimum ~$15,000/year for portfolio accounting services (historical reference)

## Target Market & Firm Size Sweet Spot
- Mid-to-large RIAs ($500M+ AUM)
- Enterprise wealth management firms
- Multi-advisor practices wanting unified operations
- C-suite executives needing performance dashboards
- NOT targeted at solo advisors or small firms

## Product Features
- **Denali AI**: Unified AI assistant across portfolio management, planning, CRM, reporting
- **Report Assistant**: AI-generated reporting
- **Query Studio**: Natural language data queries
- **Strategic Insights**: AI dashboard for C-suite (revenue, AUM, advisor performance, client behavior)
- **Upcoming**: AI proposal generation, account opening, billing, reconciliation
- Mobile and tablet apps for Denali AI
- Portfolio management, trading, rebalancing
- Performance reporting and billing
- Compliance tools
- Financial planning integration
- CRM integration (Orion Connect)
- Insurance solutions (DPL Technology integration)

## Weaknesses & Gaps (what advisors complain about)
- **Employee satisfaction crisis**: 2.4/5 Glassdoor rating from 281 reviews
- **Executive turnover**: "70% of the executive team resigned in a period of 12 months"
- **Offshoring issues**: "80% of the historic data team was replaced with an offshore team, leading to missed deadlines and expected overtime"
- **Product complaints**: Client portal has "poor layout/customization," data not cohesive across ecosystem
- **Slow integrations**: "Need more developer resources" and "slow response times"
- **Return-to-office controversy**: Poorly executed RTO policy frustrated employees
- **Pricing opacity**: No self-serve pricing; custom proposals create friction
- **Below-industry compensation**: Reported as a major employee concern
- **No content creation tools**: Denali AI focuses on data queries and reporting, not on helping advisors write emails or create marketing content

## How Advisor Intelligence Differentiates
1. **Different market segment** — Orion targets enterprise firms with $500M+ AUM. We serve solo advisors and small RIAs at $49.99/month. Zero overlap.
2. **Self-serve simplicity** — No custom proposals, no sales calls, no minimum commitments. Sign up and start using templates immediately.
3. **Content creation focus** — Denali AI queries portfolio data and generates reports. We help advisors create compelling client communications, marketing content, and prospecting materials.
4. **300x cheaper entry point** — $600/year vs ~$15,000/year minimum.
5. **Platform-agnostic** — Works regardless of whether the advisor uses Orion, Schwab, Fidelity, or any other platform.
6. **No internal chaos** — As a focused product with a clear scope, we don't suffer from the enterprise complexity and organizational issues plaguing Orion.
