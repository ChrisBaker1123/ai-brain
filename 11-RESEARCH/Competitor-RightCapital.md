# Key Takeaways for Advisor Intelligence
1. RightCapital's Smart Import (AI-powered data entry reduction by 70%) and Tax Analyzer (OCR tax return analysis) show planning software is eating adjacent features. If they add content generation, they could compete with us.
2. RightCapital's 92% user satisfaction and "always continuing to improve" reputation is the gold standard for advisor software — we should aim for the same level of responsiveness to user feedback.
3. At $125-$255/month per advisor, RightCapital's core planning software is 2.5-5x our price. Advisors already paying that will find our $49.99/mo easy to justify as an add-on.

# RightCapital

## Overview
RightCapital is the fastest-growing financial planning software, founded in 2015. Launched at XYPN conference and jumped to 4th in market share within 5 years. Known for best-in-class tax-sensitive retirement distribution planning and collaborative client interface. Continuously adding AI features including Smart Import (AI data entry) and Tax Analyzer (OCR tax return scanning).

## Pricing (Confirmed 2026)
- **Basic**: $124.95/user/month
- **Premium**: $149.95/user/month (includes account aggregation, deeper automation)
- **Platinum**: Custom pricing (firm-level oversight, everything in Premium + coordination tools)
- Annual commitment required for first year
- Consolidated billing available for 10+ advisor licenses
- Tax Analyzer bundled with Premium and Platinum subscriptions
- Smart Import: included at no additional cost for all users
- Transparent pricing with no hidden fees

## Target Market & Firm Size Sweet Spot
- Independent RIAs (primary)
- Solo advisors and small firms (sweet spot — XYPN roots)
- Fee-only financial planners
- Advisors focused on retirement decumulation and tax planning
- Firms migrating from eMoney (positioned as 50% cost savings)
- Growing into mid-size firms

## Product Features
- Comprehensive financial planning (retirement, tax, estate, insurance, Social Security, student loans)
- **Tax-sensitive decumulation planning** (key differentiator)
- **Smart Import**: AI-powered document reading, 70% reduction in data entry time
- **Tax Analyzer**: OCR tax return scanning with planning integration
- RightExpress: Single-topic financial plans for prospecting
- Collaborative client planning interface
- Roth conversion analysis
- Medicare planning
- Account aggregation (client account sync)
- Integrations with Jump and Zocks for AI meeting-to-plan updates
- Data migration tool: imports eMoney plans via PDF scanning

## Messaging & Positioning
- "Financial planning software done right"

- Cost-competitive positioning against eMoney (50% savings for 10-advisor firms)
- Language: "intuitive," "transparent," "collaborative"
- Positioned as the modern alternative to legacy planning software

## Strengths (what they do better than advisorintelligence.app)
- Comprehensive financial planning software with deep tax analysis
- AI features (Smart Import, Tax Analyzer) reduce manual work
- 92% satisfaction rating — advisors genuinely love the product
- Collaborative client interface creates engagement
- RightExpress provides prospect conversion tool
- Jump/Zocks integrations bring AI meeting data into plans
- Strong competitive positioning against eMoney (50% cheaper)
- Continuous improvement based on advisor feedback

## Weaknesses & Gaps (what advisors complain about)
- **Tax limitations**: "Not flexible when it comes to NUA taxes," Roth conversion tool "falls flat on how taxes are paid"
- **Reporting gaps**: "Nowhere near what eMoney offers" for report generation
- **Integration issues**: Doesn't integrate well with eTrade RSUs
- **Advanced planning gaps**: International planning, advanced estate planning, business owner planning, stock option analysis
- **Budget feature**: "Not as intuitive or helpful as expected"
- **No content creation**: No templates for client communication, prospecting, or marketing
- **Base case limitations**: Software assumes "simple non-dynamic withdrawal schemes for the base case"
- **Annual commitment**: Required for first year, though pricing is transparent

## How Advisor Intelligence Differentiates
1. **Content creation vs. plan creation** — RightCapital builds financial plans. We help advisors communicate about those plans, prospect clients, and create marketing content.
2. **60-80% cheaper** — $49.99/mo vs $125-$255/mo.
3. **No planning platform lock-in** — Works with RightCapital, eMoney, MoneyGuide, or no planning software at all.
4. **Zero data requirements** — RightCapital needs client financial data input. Our copy-paste templates work without any client data on our platform.
5. **Complementary tool** — RightCapital users who build great financial plans still need help explaining those plans to clients, prospecting new clients, and marketing their practice. That's us.
6. **Instant value** — No learning curve, no data migration, no account aggregation setup. Templates are ready to use in 2 minutes.
